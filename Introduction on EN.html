Welcome

You are an ordinary animal merchant located in a farm of an extraordinary farmer. Your goal today is to buy 4 bulls. To sell them to you, the farmer has a 4-digit price in his mind, and you have to guess it and offer it to him without him telling you what the price is. The farmer will accept each offer and give you 1 bull for each digit from your offer matched with the digit and the place of his price. It will also offer you 1 cow for each matching digit of your offer matched with his digit, but not matching
with the place of the digit from his price.
