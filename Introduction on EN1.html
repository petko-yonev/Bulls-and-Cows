The digit"4" of your offer coincides with the digit"4" and the places in the number are the same, so it gives you a bull. Also in your offer there is the digit "2", as well as in the farmer`s price, but their places do not match, so he gives you a cow. For all other non-matching digits, the farmer wont offer anything to you.
